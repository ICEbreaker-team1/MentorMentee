HOW TO USE THIS SITE?

STEP 1 : Create an account

All you have to do to join our family, is just to create and account. Click on the "Creat Account" option on the top part of the website,and create your account as mentor or mentees.
We appreciate your patience.We promise to not bug you with hundreds of e-mails,so please don't hesitate.

STEP 2 : Create your profile

Once you have logged into you account,fill up all the details without hesitation or fear.We strongly believe in privacy of our users.Rest assured, your information is in safe hands!

STEP 3: The easy part

Once you have filled your profile with honest and real information, just press the "Find Me A Mentor/Mentee" button on the bottom of the page.Now all you have to do is sit back,relax,maybe even sip on some coconut water and let our algorithm do the rest.We will alert you once we find you a perfect match!
